ABOUT US
"We make a living by what we get, but we make a life by what we give."
The Chogoria Tuinuane initiative is a program established to extend financial support to vulnerable learners, following a clear recognition that Chogoria Boys High School had a number of students genuinely in need. This need was intensified by the realization that the assistance previously offered on a small scale through the students’ welfare office, individual teachers, and teachers’ welfare arrangements was having only a limited and short-lived impact. At its core, the initiative seeks to ensure that the most vulnerable members of the student community access quality education without interruption. The Chogoria Tuinuane initiative was launched in May 2025 under the patronage of the Principal, Mr. Nyaga Kinyua. It was formally registered as a community-based organisation within the Department of Social Services. The initiative held its first fundraising event on 10th May 2025, drawing together stakeholders from diverse backgrounds and sectors of society.
