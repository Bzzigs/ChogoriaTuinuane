ABOUT US
"We make a living by what we get, but we make a life by what we give."
The Chogoria Boys High School Fee Fund is a dedicated initiative aimed at providing financial assistance to students in need. This fund helps ensure that every child at Chogoria Boys High School has the opportunity to continue their education without the burden of unpaid school fees. By supporting this fund, you are giving hope and changing lives, one student at a time. Established in 2024, the Chogoria Boys High School Fee Fund was inspired by similar successful programs.
The goal is to provide all students with the same opportunity to excel academically and personally, regardless of their financial background. By contributing to the Chogoria Boys High School Fee Fund, you are investing in the future of these young minds, empowering them to achieve their dreams and become responsible, educated members of society. Your generosity can make a significant difference in their lives, fostering a brighter future for the entire community.
Motto
Giving hope | Changing lives
Vision
To empower young minds in an inclusive, supportive environment to achieve their dreams and grow into responsible, educated citizens.
Mission
A future where every student enjoys equal access to education, free from financial barriers.
